Magic:  The Gathering

Magic is a card game in which you are a wizard against another wizard in control for a magical plane.  You must have a least two players but can have any number more to play.  Each player has a deck of at least 40 cards to play against the other players decks.

If you would like to learn how to play go to 5th Edition Rules for more information on how to play the game and all the rules.
