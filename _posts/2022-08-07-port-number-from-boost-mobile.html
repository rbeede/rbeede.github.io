--- creation_epoch: 1659924659 lastmod_epoch: 1659924659 categories: ['Tech Tricks'] title: --- Want to port your phone number out without calling Boost Mobile support on the phone to get your account number and port pin?
  1. Log into your boost mobile account online
  2. Navigate to https://aka-apiservices.boostmobile.com/api/prepaid/1.0/accounts/4055340105/familyplandetails?idField=mdn
  3. View the page source. In the JSON ignore "mdn"
  4. Use subscriberId for the port account number
  5. For the port PIN just use your Boost Mobile account number

I used Burp proxy to view the content and find it. Ignore other ones that say CUSTOMER_ACCOUNT_ID as that is just your phone number which is not correct. Saves you from having to hear a sales pitch and wait in the phone queue.
