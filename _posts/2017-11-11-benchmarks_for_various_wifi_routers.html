--- creation_epoch: 1498268744 lastmod_epoch: 1554999993 categories: Misc title: Benchmarks for various wifi routers --- I got some routers on clearance and wanted to benchmark them from various locations. I thought I'd publish my results in case it was useful for others.

Each router is running the OEM latest firmware at the time of testing. A gigabit fiber backbone was used with an 802.11ac wireless client 2x2 MIMO. The 5ghz spectrum (80mhz) was on a dedicated channel with no others, but 2.4ghz was in a residential neighborhood of homes with probably average 3 other signals on 1,6,11. This gives a nice average real-world comparison for typical crowded 2.4ghz.
