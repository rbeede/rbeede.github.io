Gigabyte B650M DS3H (rev 1.0)

Advanced Mode

Settings

"HD Audio Controller" means the audio 3.5mm jack ports built into the motherboard. If you don't use those you can disable this to have one less device (and a fewer drivers) in your OS.

Settings, AMD CBS

  • NBIO (North Bridge)
  • Audio configuration
  • NB Azalia
  • Allows for HDMI or DispayPort audio to monitors with speakers. At least in a GPU configuration.
