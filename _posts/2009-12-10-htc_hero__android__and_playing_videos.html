--- creation_epoch: 1260468156 lastmod_epoch: 1260468156 categories: ['Tech Tricks'] title: HTC Hero (Android) and playing videos --- So I had some videos I wanted to convert and play on my HTC Hero (Android) phone. Here is what I'm using currently:
  • Videora Android Converter - Converts many formats to the needed format with a nice GUI. Free. Just displays ads in the program to support itself.
  • Android Market app (replaced) aPlayer - Free and plays back mp4 files. Very basic player without playlist support, but it works.
  • All Programs menu, Albums - Built-in and supports mp4 format. Lacks playlists but has slideshow mode at least
