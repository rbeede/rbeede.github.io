--- creation-epoch: 1224001560 lastmod-epoch: 1224001560 categories: ['Free Stuff'] title: Free encrypted USB key drive on Windows without administrator privileges --- I found a cool freeware utility that can create an encrypted USB drive that works under Windows without administrator privileges. You can insert the USB drive (it doesn't have to be U3, any type will work), and it will have a drive letter assigned after you enter a password.

It's called Rohos Mini Drive
