--- creation_epoch: 1760400126 lastmod_epoch: 1760570397 categories: ['Tech Tricks'] title: Attempting Firmware Access to Calix GigaSpire Blast GS4220E --- I was unsuccessful at getting a TTL Serial USB UART to show the console during boot. It may be disabled or the PINs may be for JTAG instead. I did get a working GND + VCC (3.3V).

Removal of the bottom required 3 screws (under the rubber feet) and pushing some plastic clips in. I was able to do so without any plastic clips breaking.
