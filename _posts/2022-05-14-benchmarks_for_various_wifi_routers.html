--- creation_epoch: 1498268744 lastmod_epoch: 1725712742 categories: ['Tech Tricks'] title: Benchmarks for various wifi routers --- A history of router benchmarks I've collected over the years.

Each router had the OEM latest firmware at the time of testing unless otherwise noted. A gigabit connection was used with an 802.11ax wireless client 2x2 MIMO. The 5ghz spectrum was on a dedicated channel with no others, but 2.4ghz was in a residential neighborhood of homes with probably average 3 other signals on 1,6,11. This gave a nice average real-world comparison for typical crowded 2.4ghz.

iperf3 --server --interval 2 --format m

  • 'm' = Mbits/sec

uploading from client to the server

iperf3 --format m --bandwidth 10000M --interval 2 --parallel 4 --client 203.0.113.101

downloading from server to the client

iperf3 --format m --bandwidth 10000M --interval 2 --parallel 4 --client 203.0.113.101 --reverse
